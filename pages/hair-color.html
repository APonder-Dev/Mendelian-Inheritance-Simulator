Understanding Hair Color Inheritance

Hair color inheritance is determined by multiple genes, leading to a variety of hair colors in offspring. Common categories include Black, Brown, Blonde, Red, and others.

The combination of alleles from both parents determines the potential hair colors that their children may inherit.
