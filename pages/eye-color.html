Understanding Eye Color Inheritance

Eye color inheritance is influenced by multiple genes, leading to a variety of eye colors in offspring. This tester simulates possible eye colors for children based on the selected eye colors of their parents. The main categories typically include Brown, Blue, Green, and Hazel.

The combination of alleles from both parents determines the potential eye colors that their children may inherit.
